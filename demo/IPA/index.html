About the
Institute of Pacific Australia


Our Mission

To be the leading education provider for Pasifika (Maori and Pacific) peoples in Australia.

Our Vision

To be the principal service provider for Pasifika peoples in Education, Career Development, Support Services and Cultural Awareness.

Which Pathway will you choose?

OUR COURSES

Leadership & Management

Managers and supervisors have a significant leadership role within their organisation, and through this program you will gain business and operational skills through industry-focused learning.

Read more

Business

The Diploma of Business will increase your employability and can be used as a platform to help you gain entry into university studies.

Read more

Youth Work

Youth Work is an essential course for students who wish to improve their knowledge and skills in order to develop programs and services for young people to meet the social, behavioural, health, welfare, developmental and/or protection needs of young people.

Read more

Community Service

The Diploma of Community Services will prepare you to work in a role that usually involves service delivery, either direct client work and/or community education or development projects.

Read more
