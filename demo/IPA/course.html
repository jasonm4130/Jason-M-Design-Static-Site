Top Reasons to choose a

YOUTH WORK DIPLOMA

The Diploma of Youth Work is an essential course for students who wish to improve their knowledge and skills in order to develop programs and services for young people to meet the social, behavioural, health, welfare, developmental and/or protection needs of young people. This work may be undertaken through employment in community, government and/or welfare agencies. The qualification is designed to ensure that policies and guidelines of the employing organisation are implemented and includes responsibility for direction and supervision of staff, contracting external agencies and/or management of specialist services.

About IPA
