About theInstitute of Pacific Australia

We deliver courses and services that are specifically aimed at and packaged for Pasifika peoples in every state and territory in Australia. Our mission is underpinned by three core principles that distinguish the Institute of Pacific Australia.

Professional

Professionalism, timeliness, positivity and a high work ethic are all valued by prospective employers, and also by us. We commit to provide a professional service and support system to our students and expect the same from our students.

Learn More

Accredited

We team up with service providers and industry-experts that are nationally accredited and have a proven record of success Our courses are informed by industry-data and evidence-based research. All of our staff are tertiary qualified and our facilitators are industry professionals.

Learn More

Culturally-Appropriate

Given the multi-cultural nature of Australian society, we know and celebrate the uniqueness of each student. We are particularly sensitive to the strengths and needs of Pasifika students in Australia, and pride ourselves on tailoring our courses and support systems to cater for these. All of our staff are driven by a passion for serving their people.
